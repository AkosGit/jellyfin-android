My Plugins Catalog Channels

Install channels such as Trailers and Vimeo in the plugin catalog.


  • In a low bandwidth environment, limiting quality can help ensure a smooth streaming experience.
Some channels support downloading content prior to viewing. Enable this in low bandwidth enviornments to download channel content during off hours. Content is downloaded as part of the channel download scheduled task.


  • Specify a custom download path if desired. Leave empty to download to an internal program data folder.
  • Limit the size of the channel download folder.
  • Downloaded content older than this will be deleted. It will remain playable via internet streaming.
