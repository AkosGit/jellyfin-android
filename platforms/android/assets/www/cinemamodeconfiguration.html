General Cinema Mode Streaming Transcoding

Cinema mode brings the theater experience straight to your living room with the ability to play trailers and custom intros before the main feature.

Enable cinema mode for:

  • Internet trailers:
    These features require an active supporter membership and installation of the Trailer channel plugin.
    Learn more
  • Requires setup of local trailers.
    Learn more
  • A folder containing video files. A video will be randomly selected and played after trailers.
  • Trailers will only be selected with a parental rating equal to or less than the content being watched.

Individual users will have the ability to disable cinema mode within their own preferences.
